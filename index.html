how to play

step 1 press the pick catagory button

step 2.once you get the catagory click the correspanding topic card to get the topic

step 3 once you have your topic click the clock to start the timer you have 60 seconds to brainstorm a mini sermon using the topic

step 4 after the mini sermon is finished the other players vote if majority rules you get a point first to 5 wins

step 5. press the pick catagory button again to reset the timer

Rules

when drawing your card, your sermon may pull inspiration from multiple points of the topic for example: when drawing a song card your illustration may be based on lyrics or the title of the song, or with a movie the theme, a scene, lines, or characters from the movie may be used.

unlike the ten commandments we encourage stealing ( of points in this game ) want to steal a point wait for your opponent to stray off topic or use an illustration from a different topic accidently and be the first to call them out and instantly give a proper illustration to gain the point

Disclaimer: Sermon Showdown is currently set up as a hosted game the "host" is responsible for all button presses and point updates
